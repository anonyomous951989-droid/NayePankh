About Us

Who We Are & What We Stand For

NayePankh Foundation is a registered NGO driven by passionate young volunteers committed to building a more equitable and compassionate society. We work across education, healthcare, and community empowerment.

Education

Providing quality education and resources to underprivileged children, helping them build a brighter future.

Healthcare

Organizing health camps, awareness drives, and providing medical support to communities in need.

Environment

Leading environmental conservation projects including tree plantation and cleanliness drives.

Empowerment

Empowering women, youth, and marginalized communities through skill development programs.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a network of compassionate young volunteers who drive sustainable change in education, healthcare, and community development. We believe that every individual has the power to make a difference, and we provide the platform to channel that energy into meaningful action.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth. We envision a future where young people lead social transformation, and no one is left behind due to circumstances of birth. Together, we build bridges of hope and opportunity.
